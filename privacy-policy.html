Privacy Policy

TrendTrack values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide (e.g., email, username) and data from TikTok’s API (e.g., video stats, with your consent).

2. How We Use Your Data

We use data to provide analytics, improve our service, and comply with legal obligations. We do not sell your data.

3. Data Sharing

We share data only with your consent or as required by law. TikTok API data is handled per TikTok’s policies.

4. Your Rights

You can access, correct, or delete your data by contacting us at support@trendtrack.example.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

6. Contact

For questions, contact us at support@trendtrack.example.
